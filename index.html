Why Cliprr exists

Stop wasting time watching streams for clips.

If you want to grow a channel, you have to post clips. But the reality is that manual clipping takes up way too much time. Most tools either guess what a highlight is or they force you to sit there scrubbing through a six hour stream just to find one good play.

I built Cliprr because I wanted a tool that actually listens to stream data. It uses things like chat spikes and custom cooldowns to catch highlights as they happen, but it keeps you in charge of what gets saved. It is a tool designed to fit into your existing workflow without being over-complicated.
